# 🚚 CarryChain

## Decentralized Human-Powered Logistics Network

> If You're Already Going, Carry While You Go.

CarryChain is a Web3-powered decentralized logistics platform that connects people who need to send parcels with travelers who are already heading to the same destination.

Instead of using a dedicated courier, CarryChain utilizes existing human mobility to create a community-powered delivery network.

Travelers can carry parcels along their existing route and earn rewards for successful deliveries.

---

## 🌍 The Problem

Traditional logistics systems are often:

- Expensive
- Slow
- Centralized
- Inefficient
- Environmentally harmful

At the same time, millions of people travel every day with unused space in their luggage or vehicles.

CarryChain transforms this unused travel capacity into a decentralized delivery network.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

CarryChain connects:

### 📦 Sender

A person who wants to send a parcel.

⬇️

### 🚶 Traveler

A person who is already traveling to the same destination.

⬇️

### 📍 Receiver

The person who receives the parcel.

⬇️

### 🎁 Reward

The traveler receives a blockchain-based reward after successful delivery.

---

## 🔄 How It Works

1. Sender creates a delivery request.
2. Delivery details and destination are recorded.
3. A traveler going in the same direction accepts the request.
4. The parcel is delivered to the receiver.
5. Receiver confirms the delivery.
6. Smart contract releases the reward.
7. User reputation is updated on-chain.

---

## 🔐 Web3 Features

### Smart Contract Escrow

Securely manages delivery rewards and releases payment after delivery confirmation.

### CARRY Reward Token

Travelers earn CARRY tokens for completing successful deliveries.

### On-Chain Reputation

Users build trust through delivery history, ratings, and completed deliveries.

### Decentralized Architecture

The system reduces dependence on a centralized logistics authority.

### USDC Payments

Stablecoin-based payments can provide transparent and efficient settlement.
